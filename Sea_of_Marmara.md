# [[Sea_of_Marmara]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Sea_of_Marmara,35367]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Sea of Marmara, also known as the Sea of Marmora or the Marmara Sea, 
> is a small inland sea entirely within the borders of Turkey. 
> 
> It links the Black Sea and the Aegean Sea via the Bosporus and Dardanelles straits, 
> separating Turkey's European and Asian sides. 
> 
> It has an area of 11,350 km2 (4,380 sq mi), and its dimensions are 280 km × 80 km. 
> Its greatest depth is 1,370 m (4,490 ft).
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sea%20of%20Marmara)
