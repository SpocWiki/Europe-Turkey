## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Taurus Mountains (Turkish: Toros Dağları or Toroslar) 
> are a mountain complex in southern Turkey, 
> separating the [[Regions~Turkey/Mediterranean_Region|Mediterranean coastal region]] from the [[Regions~Turkey/Central_Anatolia_Region|Central_Anatolia_Region]]n Plateau. 
> 
> The system extends along a curve from Lake Eğirdir in the west 
> to the upper reaches of the [[../../../Asia/Asia~West/Euphrates|Euphrates]] and [[../../../Asia/Asia~West/Tigris|Tigris]] rivers in the east. 
> 
> It is a part of the Alpide belt in Eurasia.
>
